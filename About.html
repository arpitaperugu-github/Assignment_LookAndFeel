About Us

The website aims to empower connecting people to the nearest possible hospital based on their ZIP Code in One-Click.
The idea of creating this website came up due to the worldwide covid pandemic suffering and feeling the plight of people especially in remote areas who don't have access to accurate and immediately available information.
